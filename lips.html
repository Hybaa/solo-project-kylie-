About Us

I created Kylie Cosmetics to give my fans access to the makeup products that I love and use every single day to create my looks. Whether I’m getting my makeup done with my glam team for a shoot, or doing my own makeup at home, I love that makeup can make you feel confident and beautiful and I want to share that feeling with you guys. My new, updated line features all of my favorite iconic Lip Kit shades as well as my must-haves including blushes, bronzers, highlighters, high glosses, matte liquid lipsticks, lip blushes, brow essentials and eyeliners. Everything is clean, vegan, with ingredients that are good for your skin, but I made sure we didn’t sacrifice quality, pigmentation or performance. For me, being able to have a lasting matte lip formula that wasn’t overly drying, was so important when reformulating my Lip Kit. My new formula has 8 hour wear and lasting color but is super comfortable on the lips. I’m obsessed with it, and I know you guys are going to love it too. And this is just the start!
